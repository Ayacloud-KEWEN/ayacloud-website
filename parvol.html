ParVol

Your Smart Golf Companion

The ultimate golf assistant app designed to make every round smarter, simpler, and more fun.

With powerful GPS, AR, and AI features, ParVol replaces expensive devices and brings pro-level tools right to your phone.

Key Features

GPS Course Maps & Distance

Access 2D overhead maps for tens of thousands of courses. See your real-time position, measure any distance, and view automatic front, middle, and back green distances.

Replace expensive rangefinders and make better strategic decisions.

Digital Scorecard

An easy interface for up to 4 players. Quickly record strokes, putts, and fairway hits, with automatic calculations for score vs. par and net scores.

Streamline scoring and track performance digitally.

AR Green Distance Finder

Use your phone’s camera to see accurate distances overlaid directly on the green. AR identifies the hole and provides precise, real-time measurements.

Get more precise readings than GPS alone, perfect for your short game.

Advanced & Social Features

Personal Swing Stats

Automatically analyze your scorecard data with visual charts for average strokes, putts, GIR, and performance on Par 3, 4, and 5 holes.

Identify strengths and weaknesses for targeted practice.

Club Management

Record your clubs, track their average/max distances, and get smart recommendations based on your target distance while playing.

Make smarter club selections and improve decision-making.

Friends & Tournaments

Add friends, create mini-tournaments with live scorecards, and host private competitions in Stroke Play or Stableford formats.

Turn solo rounds into social, competitive fun.

Pro-Level Features

AR Green Reading

A revolutionary putting aid. Scan the green with your camera to display slope and break direction using heatmaps and arrows, providing a visual guide for your putt.

A revolutionary putting aid for golfers of all skill levels.

AI Rules Assistant

Get instant answers to any golf rule question using AI. It supports text, image, and voice queries, and even works offline.

Acts as a low-cost, professional rules referee right in your pocket.
