Your Ultimate Travel Companion

PinGO is the ultimate companion for every traveler, designed to help you effortlessly document your journeys with an elegant, minimalist interface. Turn your check-ins into beautiful, interactive travel memories.

Features That Map Your Memories

Intelligent Check-Ins

Simply tap to log your location, complete with precise coordinates, altitude, and a detailed address. PinGO can even automatically pull the location and date from your photos for seamless logging.

Visualize Your Journey

See your entire travel history on a single, stunning map. Switch between standard, satellite, and hybrid map styles to get the perfect view of your adventures.

AI-Powered Note Taking

Use your camera to instantly scan and digitize text from signs, menus, or monuments. You can also dictate your thoughts and memories hands-free with built-in speech recognition.

Rich Location Data

Powered by Wikidata and OpenStreetMap, PinGO provides rich, encyclopedic information about the places you visit, turning every check-in into a learning experience.

Share Your Story

Create stunning, high-resolution map snapshots of your check-ins, perfect for sharing your travel route and highlights on social media.

Personal Statistics & Achievements

Track your progress as you explore the world. See how many countries, cities, and locations you've visited, and unlock achievements as you go.

Capture Your World

Your Data, Your Control.

We believe your memories belong to you. That's why PinGO is built with privacy at its core.

Secure On-Device Storage

All your data is stored securely on your device, powered by Core Data.

Easy Data Export

Easily export your entire travel log in popular formats like KML, GeoJSON, and JSON, ensuring you always have a backup for tools like Google Earth.
