Advanced AR Platforms

Mirrorworld

Our Mirrorworld platform creates a perfect digital replica of the physical world, allowing for persistent AR experiences that multiple users can interact with simultaneously. This revolutionary technology maintains state across sessions, enabling complex applications that evolve over time. Key applications include persistent virtual art installations, location-based gaming, and design environments where changes made by one user are visible to all participants in real-time.

Immersive Map

Immersive Map transforms traditional navigation by overlaying interactive digital information directly onto the physical environment. Our platform understands terrain, buildings, and points of interest to create rich, context-aware experiences.

Beyond basic navigation, Immersive Map enables historical recreations, future city planning visualizations, and interactive tourism experiences that bring locations to life with stories, data, and digital artifacts.
