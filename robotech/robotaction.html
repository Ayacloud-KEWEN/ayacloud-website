GUARDIAN-X Jump Movement

Action Name Jump Movement
Action Type Entertainment
Duration 15 seconds
Energy Level Medium

The GUARDIAN-X jump movement showcases its exceptional balance capabilities and flexible joint control. Through precision algorithm control, the robot can smoothly execute complex jump movement sequences while maintaining a stable posture.

Technical features: Uses inverse kinematics algorithms to ensure movement fluidity, built-in gyroscope and accelerometer for real-time posture adjustment to prevent imbalance. Movement sequences are programmable and support custom choreography.
